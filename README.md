## 🪴 My Digital Garden

Welcome to my digital garden. This repository contains the production build of my personal corner of the web, where I cultivate ideas, document my technical journey, and share what I'm learning in real-time.

### Philosophy
Unlike a traditional blog, a digital garden is imperfect by design. You'll find:
* **Seedlings:** Rough notes and early-stage ideas.
* **Budding:** Topics that are starting to take shape with more research.
* **Evergreen:** Well-documented, polished pieces of knowledge.

### Built With
This site is powered by **Astro**, utilizing its "islands architecture" to keep the frontend lightweight and performant. 

* **Framework:** [Astro JS](https://astro.build/)
* **Architecture:** Static Site Generation (SSG)
